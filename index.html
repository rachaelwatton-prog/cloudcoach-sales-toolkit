Cloud Coach Sales Toolkit

Deal roles & responsibilities

Three roles work every deal together. The AE owns logistics and deal management throughout. Frank (exec sponsor) joins from demo onwards — focused on vision, executive multithreading, and commercial conversations. Christian leads all technical discovery and demo work. Critical rule: always book the next meeting before ending any call.

AE — deal management & logistics
Frank — exec sponsor, vision & commercial
Christian — technical discovery & demo
Stage 1 — Prospecting

AE

  • Cold outbound and qualify fit
  • Book and confirm discovery call
  • Log all activity to HubSpot / Salesforce

Frank — not yet involved

  • Available if a large logo or AI deal surfaces
  • Monitors Gong signals for early flags

Christian — not yet involved

  • Available to advise on technical fit questions
  • Reviews discovery questions with AE if needed
Stage 2 — Discovery

AE

  • Runs the discovery call
  • Asks structured discovery questions
  • Logs current state and pain to CRM
  • Books the demo before leaving the call

Frank — reviews async

  • Reviews call recording via Gong
  • Checks energy, tone, and discovery quality
  • Monitors signals: AI mentions, large logos, exec buyers
  • Decides whether to join the demo

Christian — advises pre-demo

  • Reviews discovery notes for technical gaps
  • Flags missing technical context before demo
  • Prepares demo environment based on prospect profile
Stage 3 — Technical Demo

AE

  • Sets up and manages the call
  • Takes notes and tracks action items
  • Books next meeting before the call ends
  • Handles all CRM updates post-call

Frank — joins live

  • Opens with product vision and roadmap narrative
  • Speaks to AI strategy and where the market is going
  • Handles exec-level and business questions
  • Does NOT answer technical questions — defers to Christian
  • Uses the call to identify other stakeholders to multithread

Christian — leads demo

  • Leads the full technical walkthrough
  • Asks and answers all technical discovery questions
  • Covers current state, integrations, and scoping
  • Owns technical objection handling throughout
Stage 4 — Working the Deal

AE

  • Manages deal mechanics end-to-end
  • Books all follow-up calls and manages cadence
  • Keeps Salesforce / MEDDPICC fields updated
  • Coordinates between Frank and Christian as needed

Frank — multithreads

  • Emails up the org chart using organic reasons (AI strategy, change management, migration complexity)
  • Gets 1:1 time with economic buyer on pricing and budget
  • Handles C-level and executive change management conversations
  • Runs Command of the Message plays on each call
  • Avoids managing the deal like an AE — that stays with the rep

Christian — technical follow-up

  • Answers outstanding technical questions
  • Works through scoping and integration detail
  • Handles technical buyer objections
Stage 5 — Negotiation & Close

AE

  • Owns contract admin and SOW
  • Manages commercials and paperwork process
  • Updates closed-won fields in Salesforce

Frank — closes on vision

  • Leads pricing negotiation
  • Reinforces roadmap commitment and strategic fit
  • Handles final exec-level hesitation
  • Confirms implementation expectations with buyer

Christian — final sign-off

  • Confirms technical fit is agreed
  • Addresses any last technical blockers
  • Hands off cleanly to onboarding / PS team
Closed won Handoff to onboarding — SOW in place, implementation tier confirmed, PS team briefed
